# 📂 Digital Diary – Case File: *The Third Angle*

> **Status:** ⚠️ Unresolved | Silence Phase  
> **Role:** Observer • Intervenor • Analyst

## 📌 Overview

This case began as a simple relationship but evolved into a **multi-layered conflict** involving:

- Harassment  
- Psychological manipulation  
- Cyber exploitation  
- Violence  
- Internal emotional collapse  

---

## 🧩 Phase 1 – The Beginning

- A new relationship (2 months old)  
- A **third person enters** with intent to connect  
- The girl **refuses and sets boundaries**

✅ *Expected Outcome:* Situation ends  
❌ *Actual Outcome:* Escalation begins  

---

## ⚠️ Phase 1 – Escalation

- Persistent attempts by the third person  
- Two more individuals join  
- Pressure tactics: *“Leave your boyfriend”*  
- Direct threats issued  

➡️ Shift: **Interest → Harassment & Intimidation**

---

## 🛑 Phase 1 – Violence & Breach

- Physical confrontation (minor but critical)  
- Unauthorized **video recording & circulation**

🚨 Result:
- Privacy violation  
- Psychological trauma  

---

## 🧠 Impact Analysis

- Multiple **panic attacks**  
- Emotional breakdown  
- Fear and social pressure
