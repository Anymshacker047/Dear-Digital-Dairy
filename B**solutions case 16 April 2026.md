📓 Dear Digital Diary – The Fake Internship Case
A real investigation. A real lesson. A real transformation.
🗓️ Date: April 16, 2026
🧩 Overview
This wasn’t just another day.
This was a multi-day investigation that started with a small doubt…
and ended with a fully exposed scam.
🔍 Day 1 – The Doubt
Everything looked professional.
Clean process
Proper communication
Structured onboarding
But something felt off.
“Not everything that looks real is real.”
That instinct was the beginning.
⚠️ Day 2 – First Crack
Spoke to a former employee.
He was terminated for “bad behaviour”
just because he refused to share confidential information from another company.
That didn’t feel right.
“Sometimes the problem isn’t the person… it’s the system.”
💥 Day 3 – Reality Hit + Personal Distraction
Investigation revealed:
Fake email system
Fake office address
No physical existence
No local recognition
“The company didn’t exist in reality.”
At the same time, a personal moment hit:
She said,
“I like someone else.”
For a moment, everything paused.
“While I was uncovering a fake system… reality hit me personally too.”
But I chose focus.
🧑‍💻 Day 4 – Victim Statements
Connected with multiple interns.
Common pattern:
Fake promises
No payments
Sudden exits
Frustration and anger
“This wasn’t random. This was structured.”
📄 Day 5 – Final Proof
Analyzed the certificate:
Created in basic Word format
Fake QR code
Same authority name: Aditi Arora
Identical joining letter patterns
“Mass-produced deception.”
⚖️ Action Taken
Reported to Cyber Crime ✔
Filed in Consumer Court ✔
“Truth was no longer just known… it was documented.”
🏁 Status
Case Closed
🧠 Bhagavad Gita Insight
श्लोक:
कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन।
Meaning:
(You have the right to perform your duty, but not to the results of your actions.)
Focus on action. Let the outcome follow.
💭 Reflection
This case taught me two things:
The world isn’t always what it shows
And not everything we want is meant for us
“Clarity may hurt, but it always helps.”
🔥 Key Learnings
Verify before trusting
Patterns reveal truth
Proof is power
Emotions can wait, truth cannot
📌 Final Thought
“I didn’t fall for the scam… I understood it.”
